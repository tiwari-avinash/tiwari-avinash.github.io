Services

Graphical DSL Design & Development#

Design visual, domain-specific languages that let your teams express business logic — pricing rules, workflow configurations, offer definitions — without writing code. Build self-service rules engines where business users author discounting logic, eligibility criteria, or approval workflows visually — and deploy changes in minutes, not sprints. Typical result: time-to-market for a new discount rule drops from weeks to 15 minutes.

Low-Code Platform Architecture#

Architect internal low-code platforms that shrink the team needed for routine feature delivery. When the tooling is right, work that required 25 developers and 2 weeks can be done by 2 people in 1 day.

Modernization for Self-Service#

Refactor legacy systems so that configuration, rules, and business logic are extracted into visual, auditable, and version-controlled layers — reducing developer dependency and accelerating change.
